## Overview

The Brief Builder transforms unstructured notes, meeting summaries, and scattered ideas into polished, structured project briefs ready for client review or internal kickoff.

## How It Works

1. **Paste your notes** — Drop in raw meeting notes, email threads, or bullet points.
2. **Select brief type** — Choose from creative brief, technical brief, production brief, or general project brief.
3. **Generate** — The AI organizes your content into a structured document.
4. **Review and edit** — Adjust sections, add details, and finalize.

## Brief Structure

Every generated brief follows PC's standard format:

- **Project Overview** — What and why
- **Objectives** — Key goals and success metrics
- **Scope** — What's included and what's not
- **Timeline** — Key milestones and deadlines
- **Team** — Roles and responsibilities
- **Technical Requirements** — Specs, platforms, integrations
- **Budget Considerations** — High-level budget notes
- **References** — Mood boards, inspiration, past work

## Tips

- The more context you provide, the better the output
- Include any client-specific language or requirements
- Use the brief as a starting point — always add your expertise

## Inputs

| Parameter | Type | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| `notes` | `string` | Raw notes, ideas, or bullet points |
| `client` | `string` | Client name for context |
| `projectType` | `string` | Type of project (event, campaign, etc.) |
