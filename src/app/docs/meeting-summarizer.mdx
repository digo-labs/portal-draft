## Overview

The Meeting Summarizer extracts structured summaries from meeting notes or transcripts. Paste your raw notes and get organized output with decisions made, action items, owners, and deadlines.

## How It Works

1. **Paste notes or transcript** — Drop in your raw meeting content.
2. **Select meeting type** — Choose from kickoff, status update, creative review, client call, or retrospective.
3. **Generate** — The AI creates a structured summary.
4. **Share** — Copy the summary to share with your team.

## Output Format

Every summary includes:

- **Attendees** — Who was in the meeting
- **Key Decisions** — What was decided
- **Discussion Points** — Main topics covered
- **Action Items** — Tasks with owners and due dates
- **Open Questions** — Unresolved items that need follow-up
- **Next Steps** — What happens next

## Tips

- Include speaker names in your notes for better action item assignment
- The more detail in your notes, the better the summary
- Use for both internal team meetings and client calls

## Inputs

| Parameter | Type | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| `transcript` | `string` | Meeting notes or transcript text |
| `format` | `summary \| action-items \| both` | Output format |
