## Overview

The Brand Voice Writer generates copy that sounds like Production Club — bold, creative, and confident. Whether you need a social media caption, an email to a client, or a project description, this skill adapts PC's tone to the format.

## How It Works

1. **Select a format** — Choose from social media post, email, proposal intro, project description, or press release.
2. **Provide context** — Paste your brief, notes, or key points.
3. **Generate** — The AI writes copy in PC's brand voice.
4. **Refine** — Edit the output or regenerate with tweaks.

## Use Cases

- **Social media** — Instagram captions, LinkedIn posts, Twitter threads
- **Client emails** — Professional outreach with PC's creative edge
- **Proposals** — Opening paragraphs that set the right tone
- **Project descriptions** — Internal and external-facing summaries

## Brand Voice Guidelines

PC's brand voice is:

- **Confident** — We know what we do and we do it well
- **Creative** — We push boundaries and think differently
- **Approachable** — We're serious about our work, not about ourselves
- **Technical** — We speak the language of production and technology

## Inputs

| Parameter | Type | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| `content` | `string` | Raw text or notes to rewrite in brand voice |
| `format` | `social \| email \| proposal \| description` | Output format for the generated copy |
| `tone` | `professional \| casual \| energetic` | Tone override (defaults to PC brand voice) |
